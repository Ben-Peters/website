Welcome Class of 2022!

Lens and Lights is a WPI student organization that specializes in providing quality event production services to the campus and having fun doing it. No experience necessary!

How to Join

We encourage you to stop by and chat or help out at any of our events. You can also stop by one of our general body meetings, which are held every Wednesday at 5PM in AK233, or visit our office on the third floor of the campus center.
