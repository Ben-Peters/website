Wall of Sound

In order to give back to our community, Lens and Lights sets up an annual fundraising event where we set up all of our speakers and play music on the Quad. This year, all proceeds will be going to Dogs for the Deaf. Anyone from the WPI community can pay $1.00 for one song of their choice to be played or $3.00 for 2 minutes of silence. Note: Lens and Lights reserves the right to deny or stop any song.

LNL will also be serving FREE food starting at 11am.
