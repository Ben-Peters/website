Online Workorder

Please complete this form as fully as possible. Once you have finished, click on the Send to LnL button at the bottom of the form and your request for services will be sent to Lens and Lights. Upon approval of your request, you will receive a confirmation via email, so be sure to fill in your email address below or a confirmation will not be sent and the work order not processed. Please note that inadequately completed work orders will also not be processed.

If you wish to file more than one work order, simply click on the Erase all fields button at the bottom of the form, and the fields will clear.

Before you start, you might want to look over our online price sheet.

Certain events require different setups, and sometimes it's hard to describe exactly what you need. Click on one of the pictures below for a floor plan of the various buildings around campus where we normally do events. Once open, feel free to either edit them in Photoshop and email them to us (lnl@wpi.edu), or print them out and write on them, then turn them into our box located in the SAO office of the Campus Center.

Important:
Failure to guarantee receipt of this request for services by the Lens and Lights Club at least two (2) weeks prior to the event may result in the following late fees being charged:

  • Less than 2 weeks notice: $25.00
  • Less than 72 hours notice: $50.00
Any requests received less than 48 hours prior to the event will not be guaranteed services.
